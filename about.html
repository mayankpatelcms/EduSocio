- Our Mission -

To provide the homework to the absenties.

About

Education is all about learning and gaining information from the teachers and other materials but the classwork & homework concept only kills the time for revising and discussing your subjects with your teachers. Our website makes it easy for the students to give more time to study. Our site has all our notebook works of all classes,section with the recommendation to their subjects teachers so that we can complete our notebook work whenever,where ever we want and submit the notebooks to teachers on selected days. With it teachers can relax and students can study almost whole year

What we wanted to do! - Suppose that a student is absent and the subject teacher has given a lot of work and he/she said to collect the all copies tomorrow. (It happens sometime) and the student don't know the thing what to do. Maybe if he asked on Whatsapp group for work. He didn't get whole thing and maybe sometimes no one replies and next day what happens you all know.
